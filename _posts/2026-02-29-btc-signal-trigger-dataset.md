## 🔍 Key Insights

<div class="insight-box">
    <i class="fas fa-chart-pie"></i>
    <h3>Statistical Analysis</h3>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Average correction time:</strong> ~8-12 hours from signal trigger</li>
        <li><strong>Average correction magnitude:</strong> 3-5%</li>
        <li><strong>Most reliable signals:</strong> Occur during extreme fear/greed extremes</li>
        <li><strong>Funding rate shifts:</strong> From positive to negative often precede corrections</li>
        <li><strong>Exchange inflows:</strong> Spike 4-6 hours before major drops</li>
    </ul>
</div>

### Pattern Recognition

1. **Pre-Signal Patterns**
   - Volume divergence 2-4 hours before signal
   - Funding rate peaking then declining
   - Whale movement to exchanges

2. **Signal Characteristics**
   - Clear catalyst (news/event)
   - Multiple timeframe alignment
   - On-chain confirmation

3. **Post-Signal Behavior**
   - Initial 2-4% move within 6 hours
   - Extended move to 5%+ within 24 hours
   - Volume confirmation required

## 🎯 Trading Implications

    <p><strong>For Traders:</strong> Use this dataset to understand typical signal patterns and improve your entry/exit timing.</p>

### Best Practices

1. **Wait for confirmation** - Don't enter on the first signal alone
2. **Check multiple timeframes** - Align 15m, 1h, and 4h charts
3. **Monitor volume** - Should increase during the move
4. **Set proper stops** - 2-3% below/above entry depending on volatility
5. **Scale in/out** - Don't enter full position at once
