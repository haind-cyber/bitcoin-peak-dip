## What is an Early Warning System?

Similar to earthquake detection systems that sense seismic activity before major tremors, Bitcoin PeakDip's Early Warning System (EWS) monitors market data to identify potential local peaks and dips before they occur.

<div class="info-box">
    <i class="fas fa-lightbulb"></i>
    <p><strong>Key Concept:</strong> The EWS doesn't predict the future - it detects patterns that historically precede significant price movements.</p>
</div>

## The 4-Layer Architecture

### Layer 1: Sensor Network
Multiple algorithms act as sensors monitoring:

- Real-time price movements from multiple exchanges
- Trading volume patterns and anomalies
- Order book dynamics and liquidity
- Derivatives data (futures, funding rates, open interest)

### Layer 2: AI Noise Filtering
Just as earthquake sensors filter out background noise, our AI algorithms:

- Remove random price fluctuations (market noise)
- Extract meaningful patterns
- Identify statistical anomalies
- Calculate confidence scores
